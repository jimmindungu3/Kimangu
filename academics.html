Academic Life

Empowering students through a rich curriculum, competent faculty, and modern resources.

Curriculum Overview

Junior School (Form 1-2)

Focused on foundational subjects including Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Integrated Science, and Creative Arts.

Senior School (Form 3-4)

Specialization begins in sciences, humanities, and technical subjects. Students prepare for KCSE with mentorship and intensive revision.

Academic Excellence

Consistently ranked among top schools in Rongai sub-county. Our students achieve B+ and above in national exams.

Top KCSE Scores

Over 75% of students score C+ and above annually.

Dedicated Faculty

Qualified teachers offering subject mastery and personal mentorship.

Innovative Learning

ICT integration, group learning, and practical-based instruction.
