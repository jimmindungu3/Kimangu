Introduction

Kimangu Day Secondary School ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our website or interact with us.

Last updated: May 15, 2025

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, email address, phone number, and other contact details when you register for admissions, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us.
  • Student Information: Academic records, attendance, behavior reports, and health information necessary for educational purposes.
  • Parent/Guardian Information: Contact details and relationship to the student.
  • Website Usage Information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process admissions applications and manage student records
  • To communicate important school updates, events, and newsletters
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer service
  • To improve our website and educational services
  • To comply with legal obligations and educational regulations
  • To ensure the safety and security of students and staff

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • School staff and teachers who need access to fulfill their responsibilities
  • Service providers who assist with website hosting, email communications, and other necessary services
  • Educational authorities and government agencies as required by law
  • Emergency services when necessary for the vital interests of students

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, accidental loss, alteration, or disclosure. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about how you use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

As an educational institution, we collect information about students under the age of 18. We only collect this information with parental/guardian consent or as permitted by applicable education and child protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on our website with the effective date.

Frequently Asked Privacy Questions

How do you protect student information?

We maintain strict security protocols for all student records. Access is limited to authorized staff members only, and all electronic records are protected with encryption and secure password policies. Physical records are kept in locked cabinets with restricted access.

Do you share student photos on social media?

We only share student photos with explicit parental consent. During enrollment, parents/guardians are asked to complete a media release form indicating their preferences regarding the use of their child's image in school publications, website, and social media.

How long do you keep student records?

We retain student records in accordance with educational regulations and data protection laws. Academic records are typically kept for 7 years after a student leaves the school. Basic enrollment information may be kept permanently as part of our historical archives.
