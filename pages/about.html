Our Story

Founded in 2016, Kimangu Day Secondary School has grown from humble beginnings with just 33 students and 4 teachers to become one of the leading academic institutions in Rongai Sub County, Nakuru.

Our school was established with a vision to provide quality education to the youth of Rongai and its surrounding areas, empowering them to become responsible citizens and future leaders of our nation.

Over the years, we have consistently maintained high academic standards while focusing on character development, leadership skills, and nurturing of talents. Today, we pride ourselves in providing holistic education to over 500 students guided by our core values of excellence, integrity, and responsibility.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To nurture responsible and disciplined students through quality education, fostering critical thinking skills, and promoting holistic development to prepare them for future challenges.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence and moral integrity in Kenya and beyond, producing well-rounded graduates who will positively impact their communities.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical conduct
  • Discipline: Self-control and adherence to rules
  • Responsibility: Taking ownership of actions and decisions
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and new ideas
