Admissions

Enroll your child in our narturing, disciplined and academically vibrant environment.

How to Apply

1. Get Application Form

Download or collect the form at our school office.

2. Submit Documents

Attach KCPE results, birth certificate, and recent passport photo.

3. Interview & Admission

Successful applicants are invited for interview and orientation.

Admission Requirements

  • KCPE Result Slip
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Two Passport Size Photos
  • Filled Application Form
  • Previous school release letter (if applicable)
  • Previous school report form (if applicable)
