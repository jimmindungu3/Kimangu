Academic Requirements

KCPE Results

Students must have completed their Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) with a minimum score of 200 marks.

We accept results from 2019 to 2024. Students with higher scores will have priority consideration.

KCPE Index Number

Valid KCPE Index Number is required for verification and record keeping.

This must match the name on the KCPE result slip and birth certificate.

Age Requirement

Students must be between 14-22 years old (born between 2003-2011).

Age verification will be done using the birth certificate.

NEMIS Number

Valid National Education Management Information System (NEMIS) number from previous school.

This is required for official school records and government reporting.
