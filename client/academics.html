Our Academic Framework

Kimangu Day Secondary School is structured around six specialized academic departments, each led by experienced Heads of Department. Our framework is designed to deliver the CBC curriculum effectively while fostering expertise in specific learning areas.

We maintain a student-centered approach with small class sizes averaging 30 students per class, ensuring personalized attention and quality instruction. Each department operates with dedicated resources, modern facilities, and continuous professional development for our teaching staff.

Our academic program is aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) requirements, focusing on developing skills, knowledge, and values that prepare students for higher education, vocational training, and future careers.

Academic Vision

To provide specialized, quality education that nurtures critical thinking, innovation, and lifelong learning skills in every student.

Teaching Approach

Collaborative, learner-centered instruction that adapts to individual needs and prepares students for real-world challenges.
