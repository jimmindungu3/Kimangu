Our Story

Founded in 1985, Kimangu Day Secondary School has grown from humble beginnings with just 45 students and 4 teachers to become one of the leading academic institutions in Nakuru County, Kenya.

Our school was established with a vision to provide quality education to the youth of Rongai and its surrounding areas, empowering them to become responsible citizens and future leaders of our nation.

Over the years, we have consistently maintained high academic standards while focusing on character development, leadership skills, and nurturing of talents. Today, we pride ourselves in providing holistic education to over 800 students guided by our core values of excellence, integrity, and responsibility.

Mr. David Kimani

Principal, Kimangu Day Secondary School

Our Journey Through The Years

1985

Foundation

Kimangu Day Secondary School was established with 45 students and 4 dedicated teachers.

1995

First Major Expansion

The school expanded with new classrooms and science laboratories, accommodating 250 students.

2005

Academic Excellence

Kimangu Day achieved top rankings in KCSE exams within Nakuru County for the first time.

2015

Technology Integration

Introduction of computer labs and digital learning resources to enhance education quality.

2025

Today's Excellence

Now with over 800 students, modern facilities, and consistent academic performance.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To nurture responsible and disciplined students through quality education, fostering critical thinking skills, and promoting holistic development to prepare them for future challenges.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence and moral integrity in Kenya and beyond, producing well-rounded graduates who will positively impact their communities.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical conduct
  • Discipline: Self-control and adherence to rules
  • Responsibility: Taking ownership of actions and decisions
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and new ideas

Our Facilities
