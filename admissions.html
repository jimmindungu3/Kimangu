Admissions

Enroll your child in a nurturing, disciplined, and academically vibrant environment.

How to Apply

1. Get Application Form

Download or collect the form at our school office.

2. Submit Documents

Attach KCPE results, birth certificate, and recent passport photo.

3. Interview & Admission

Successful applicants are invited for interview and orientation.

Admission Requirements

  • KCPE Result Slip
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Two Passport Size Photos
  • Filled Application Form
  • Previous school report form (if applicable)

Fees Structure Summary

The average termly fee is approximately KES 15,000. This includes tuition, activities, and exam fees.

Full breakdown available at the office or upon request.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is the next intake?

A: January and May every year.

Q: Is there boarding?

A: No, we are a day school but support transport arrangements.

Q: Can I apply online?

A: Not yet. Forms are collected physically from the school office.
