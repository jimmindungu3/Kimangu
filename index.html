Why Choose Kimangu Day Secondary School

Academic Excellence

Consistently top-performing school in KCSE exams with qualified and dedicated teachers.

Modern Facilities

Well-equipped laboratories, digital library, computer labs, and sports amenities.

Character Formation

Strong emphasis on discipline, leadership skills, and moral integrity.

Diverse Co-Curriculars

Sports, music, drama, clubs, and societies for holistic student development.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Kimangu Day Secondary School

Our Mission

To nurture responsible and disciplined students through quality education, fostering critical thinking skills, and promoting holistic development to prepare them for future challenges.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence and moral integrity in Kenya and beyond, producing well-rounded graduates who will positively impact their communities.

Our Motto

"Discipline. Determination. Destiny."
We believe that discipline and determination are key ingredients to shaping one's destiny for greatness.
